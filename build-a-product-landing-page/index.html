Information

Glass Beams is an Australian musical trio from Melbourne, Australia. The band was founded by Indian-Australian multi-instrumentalist and producer Rajan Silva in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic, with two other unnamed members. The band blends Eastern musical elements with contemporary Western music.

History

Glass Beams' debut EP, Mirage, was released in 2021. The follow-up EP, Mahal, released on Ninja Tune on in March 2024, entering at #5 in the Billboard Contemporary Jazz Albums chart and #13 on the Billboard Jazz Albums. In all public appearances as a group, the band wears gold bejeweled glass masks.
